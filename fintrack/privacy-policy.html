Privacy Policy for FinTrack

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Introduction

FinTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture (if available)
  • Google account ID

Financial Data

  • Transaction records you create
  • Budget information
  • Categories and expense tracking data
  • Credit card expense records

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your identity
  • Sync your financial data across devices via Google Drive
  • Provide personalized expense tracking features
  • Maintain and improve our services

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All financial data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
  • Your data remains under your control

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • When you enable cloud sync, your data is backed up to your personal Google Drive
  • We use Google Drive API with limited scope access
  • Only you can access your backed-up data through your Google account
  • We do not store your data on our servers

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication
  • Google Drive API: For cloud backup (optional)

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Share your personal information with third parties
  • Sell your data
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Access your data for any purpose other than providing the service

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Export your data in CSV, JSON, PDF, or Excel format
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of cloud sync features

Children's Privacy

FinTrack is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
